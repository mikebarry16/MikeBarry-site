WORK INFO

GRINCH x BLOOMINGDALES (2018) – Central to the promotional partnership between Bloomingdales and The Grinch was an opportunity to feature our film in the iconic Lexington Avenue windows at the flagship 59th street location. Intended to be a visually spectacular and joyful display, the windows loosely juxtaposed the Grinch’s cold, sharp lair with the warm, jubilant town of Whoville. With help from the brilliant team at Spaeth, many of the films’ Seussian gadgets were brought to life and reimagined, including an elaborate coffee press, a telescope into Whoville, a gift-wrapping machine and a contorting brass organ that extended outside the windows. Bookending the design were interactive elements – on one end was an imposing 8 foot Grinch head that judged pedestrians on their “resting Grinch face”, while the other featured a bespoke, functioning karaoke machine loaded with holiday classics.
