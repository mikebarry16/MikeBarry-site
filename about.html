MIKE BARRY
WORK INFO

Mike Barry is a graphic designer, artist and illustrator based in New York. Currently, he is a design director for Illumination Labs, the brand studio of Illumination Entertainment, creators of the highest-grossing animated film franchise of all time, Despicable Me.

Mike received a BA in Architecture Design from Yale University in 2009.
