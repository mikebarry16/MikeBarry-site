McDonald's x Minions
Design Direction, Concept Art

Creative direction, concept art, toy design for over a decade of promotional partnerships between the Minions franchise and McDonald’s. Selected work below for 2022’s release of Minions: Rise of Gru, a program which appeared in over 70 international markets and was centered around 4 distinct lines of happy meal toys and included design for a multitude of custom packages, bags, POS and digital/social. The 2020 program for Minions featured a never-been-done-before offering of over 100 unique collectible minion figures.
