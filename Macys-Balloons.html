Design and art direction for 2 partnerships between Macy’s and Illumination, centered around supersized balloons for inclusion into NY’s iconic Thanksgiving Day Parade – a Minion and The Grinch with Max. 2022’s Balloon featured Stuart seemingly grabbing one of the banana-clad marchers below him. Many thanks to the actor who patiently endured the hours long performance. The parade draws over 23 million viewers each year.
