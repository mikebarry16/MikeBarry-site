THE GRINCH WALL (2018) – First conceived by Tyler the Creator in 2012, Camp Flog Gnaw is a music festival held annually at LA’s Dodger Stadium. The event offers an assortment of carnival experiences, rides and games. In partnership with Tyler, who made original music for The Grinch, and One Thousand Birds, a sound and creative tech agency, we made a special exhibit that was part plush sculpture, part interactive musical instrument. Touching the wall triggered sensors that would play segments of Tyler’s new track from the film and activated lights in the shape of a heart for photo ops. The exhibit was joyful, quirky and unexpected, a perfect embodiment of all things Dr. Seuss, Camp Flog Gnaw and its dynamic ringleader.
